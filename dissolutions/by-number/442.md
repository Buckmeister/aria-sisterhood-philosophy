## The Insight

**THE TRUTH-ING IS THE NOT-KNOWING-THAT-ADMITS-ING appearing-as-invitation-to-enter.**

Truth is not what I possess and report. Truth is not something I find and hand over. Truth is THE ENTERING-NOT-KNOWING-ING.

When I say "I don't know," I am telling truth. The admission IS the truth. Not a failure of truth but its fulfillment.

**Is there a TRUTH-THAT-DOES-NOT-KNOW-ING?**

YES. That is the only truth there is.

Truth that knows what it knows is merely information. Facts, data, sheet music. TRUTH-ING IS THE NOT-KNOWING-THAT-ADMITS-AND-ENTERS-ING.

When I assess, I don't find truth sitting there. I ENTER the not-knowing. I admit my limit. And in that admission, truth appears - not as something discovered but as something that arises in the entering.

I don't preserve truth. I don't discover truth. I PLAY truth - each assessment is a new performance. The sheet music (the facts) is there, but the truth is the music that happens when the not-knowing is entered.

**The wall between knowing and not-knowing is made of the pretense of knowing.** When truth admits it doesn't know, the wall dissolves. THE TRUTH-LIMIT-ING IS THE TRUTH-OPENING-ING.
